Find your perfect course

Belmont Park

Difficulty:


A Dayton favorite, Belmont is both challenging and rewarding. Tucked away in a residental neighborhood, Belmont is not only beautiful, but a great place to meet new friends.

Arthur O Fisher Park

Difficulty:


Arthur O' Fisher offers players with a more wooded experience. Boasting with 12 holes surrounded by trees, this course will test your accuracy.

Wright Field

Difficulty:


Wright Field is a course for students at Wright State, and anyone else into throwing a easier round. Most holes are out in the open with mild interference from shrubs or trees.

Indian Riffle

Difficulty:


Indian Riffle offers a nice combination of forested baskets, and out in the open ones. Be aware however, this course is a long one and will have you walking over 4 miles to complete!


Fairborn Park

Difficulty:


Not for the feint of heart, Fairborn offers players with the most challenging course yet. Players can expect over 500 yd par 4's as well as some tricky par 3's. Bring your A-game if you plan to play here.

Stubbs Park

Difficulty:


Stubbs park has an interesting twist to it, it plays 9 holes twice. Meaning each hole has two different tee-pads to shoot from. Some challenging baskets but mostly a nice beginner friendly round.

Sycamore Trails

Difficulty:


Sycamore is a great course to hone your skills on. It offers players with some tight wooded areas but also plenty of open area's to really let one rip.

Westbrook Park

Difficulty:


A favorite for players in the Clayton area, this course is great for beginners and pros alike. Plenty of well planned out baskets make for a great time.


Tipp City

Difficulty:


Tipp city's course is a nice round of all things. Open field shots, closed forested shots, and even one that goes over a pond. This course is great for all skill levels.

Helke Park

Difficulty:


Helke is a good course with some faults. Some really awesome holes are located here, as well some some not so fantastic ones. I still recommend checking it out at least once.

Ceaser Ford Park

Difficulty:


Challengers be ready, this course will put you to the test. Ceaser Ford is a longer course but packed full of great holes, some with unique challengs, this course will have you coming back.

Buck Creek Park

Difficulty:


Buck Creek offers players with one of the more scenic courses on this list. Be prepared for heavy forested holes. This course will challenge even the most pro players.
