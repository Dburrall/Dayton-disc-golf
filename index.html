Let's Play

Dayton is known for all types of things: fine dining, a great night life & historic tourist attractions. There is another thing that gets overlooked: It's amazing Disc Golf Community.

You may not know this, but within 25 mile radius of Dayton there are over 50 courses to choose from. That's right FIFTY

Not to mention we have some of the best shops and community to help you get into Disc Golf. With stores like Hazy Shade that are dedicated Disc Golf stores, you can pick up some discs and advice.
