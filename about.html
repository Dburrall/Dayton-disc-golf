ABOUT

A Dayton native with a love for Disc Golf

It all started 7 years ago back in 2015 when a few friends asked me to come play at the Kettering Rec centers course.

It started off terrible as I immediately lost my friends driver right into a tree line, but I was hooked from that moment on. From there I hit up my local shop and began getting discs from the advice of my friends in hopes to hit par one day

Flash Forward a few years and I found myself playing at every course in the tri-state area, even getting some birdies a long the way. I've made dozens of friends, had some amazing experiences, and continue to grow better every season
